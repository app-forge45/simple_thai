# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 09.09.2025

## 1. Information We Collect
- The app stores the sentences, words, and ratings that you enter locally on your device.  
- No personal information (like your name, email, or phone number) is collected or sent to any server.  
- If you choose to contact us via email, your email address will only be used to respond to your message.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- All data remains on your device and is used solely to track your learning progress within the app.  
- We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

## 3. Analytics (Optional)
- If we collect anonymized usage statistics to improve the app, this data will **not include any personal identifiers**.

## 4. Data Control
- You can delete sentences, words, and ratings at any time from within the app.  
- Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be available in the app or at the GitHub Pages URL where this file is hosted.
