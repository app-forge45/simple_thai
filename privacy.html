Privacy Policy for Simple Thai

Last updated: November 13, 2025

Simple Thai values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android app Simple Thai handles information and protects your privacy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you:

  • No login or registration is required.
  • We do not collect your name, email, location, or any other identifying information.
  • We do not track your activity across apps or websites.

You can use Simple Thai completely offline without providing any personal data.

2. Local Data Storage

All your learning data — such as Thai words, sentences, and your progress — is stored only on your device using a local SQLite database:

  • Your data never leaves your device.
  • You can reset your learning data at any time within the app.
  • If you uninstall the app, all data is automatically deleted.

3. Connection to Supabase

The app connects to Supabase approximately every two days to check for new Thai words and sentences:

  • No user data is uploaded or shared.
  • The connection is read-only and used only to download new learning material.
  • Supabase does not receive any identifying information about users.

4. Advertising

The app displays **non-personalized banner ads** provided by third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob):

  • Ads are based only on the app content and do not use your personal data or activity history.
  • We do not collect or store any information about you for ads.

Learn more about Google’s advertising policies here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. Text-to-Speech

Simple Thai uses the device’s built-in Text-to-Speech (TTS) functionality to read words and sentences aloud:

  • This feature runs entirely on your device.
  • No data is sent to any servers when using TTS.
  • Your learning progress or any personal data is not accessed by the TTS service.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no central database or cloud server that could expose your information. Your learning progress remains fully on your device.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted with a revised “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: appforge45@gmail.com
