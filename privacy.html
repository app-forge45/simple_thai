Privacy Policy for Simple Thai

Last updated: November 13, 2025

Simple Thai values user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Android app Simple Thai handles information and protects privacy.

1. Information Not Collected

Simple Thai does not collect, store, or share any personal information:

  • No login or registration is required.
  • The app does not collect names, email addresses, location data, or other identifying information.
  • User activity across apps or websites is not tracked.

The app can be used completely offline without providing any personal data.

2. Local Data Storage

All learning data — such as Thai words, sentences, and progress — is stored only on the device using a local SQLite database:

  • Data never leaves the device.
  • Learning data can be reset at any time within the app.
  • Uninstalling the app automatically deletes all stored data.

3. Connection to External Database

Simple Thai connects to an external database approximately every two days to check for new Thai words and sentences:

  • No user data is uploaded or shared.
  • The connection is read-only and used only to download new learning material.
  • The database does not receive any identifying information about users.

4. Advertising

The app displays non-personalized banner ads provided by third-party ad networks (Google AdMob):

  • Ads are based only on app content and do not use personal data or activity history.
  • No information is collected or stored for advertising purposes.

Learn more about Google’s advertising policies here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. Text-to-Speech

Simple Thai uses the device’s built-in Text-to-Speech (TTS) functionality to read words and sentences aloud:

  • This feature runs entirely on the device.
  • No data is sent to any servers during TTS usage.
  • Learning progress or any personal data is not accessed by the TTS service.

6. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no central database or cloud server that could expose information. Learning progress remains fully on the device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted with a revised “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
